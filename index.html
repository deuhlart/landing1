What is this ?!

The concept is really simple we propose you to taste quality beers in a simply magnificent place, where you will be able to see all type of animals but especially bears. But it's not only for adults, you can also bring your children to enjoy the place.

Who is here?

If you come to our beautiful natural park you will have the chance to see different animals in an exceptional environment, but with a little luck you will be able to distinguish our 3 mascots. Who are without pretense the most splendid bears in the world (as long as nobody disputes this title).

This is Palou, probably the most adorable bear you will ever see.

Bowser the most imposing and terrifying bear of the parc.

And here is the only polar bear of our reserve that you will be able to see if you take our boat which will take you in a more adapted environment.

why come and visit us?

We are a beautiful and natural park where the animals are free to roam. We offer you to taste delicious original beer in a splendid environment.
